Privacy Policy

Nuran — Talk & Learn  ·  Effective July 10, 2026

The short version: this app collects nothing. No accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, no data transmission of any kind. Everything the child or caregiver does stays on the device.

Data collection

Nuran collects no personal data and transmits no data. The app has no server, no login, no analytics library, and no third-party code. It works entirely offline. We, the developers, receive nothing about you, your child, or your use of the app — by design, we could not see it even if we wanted to.

What stays on the device

Words, categories, photos, voice recordings, settings, and a local log of which word buttons were tapped (used only to show caregivers a simple progress view inside the app) are stored locally on the device. This information never leaves the device unless a caregiver deliberately uses the Back up button, which creates a file the caregiver controls and chooses where to save or send. Deleting the app deletes all of it.

Microphone and camera

The microphone is used only when a caregiver chooses to record their voice for a word or a name. The camera and photo library are used only when a caregiver chooses to add a photo to a word or a person. Recordings and photos are stored on the device and are never uploaded anywhere. Both permissions are optional; the app works without them.

Children's privacy

Nuran is made for children who use augmentative and alternative communication (AAC). Because the app collects and transmits nothing, no personal information about any child is ever gathered by us or shared with anyone. There are no ads, no in-app purchases, no links to social media, and no external content.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be published at this address and in the project repository. Because the app's design makes data collection impossible without a complete rewrite, meaningful changes are unlikely.

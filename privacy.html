Privacy Policy

Nuran — Talk & Learn  ·  Effective July 19, 2026

The short version: Nuran does not collect or upload your data. No accounts, analytics, advertising, or tracking. Child and caregiver data stays on the device unless a caregiver deliberately exports and shares a backup file.

Data collection

Nuran collects no personal data and sends no user data to the developers or third parties. The app has no server, login, analytics library, advertising SDK, or third-party service. The web version downloads its app files during installation and updates; after that it works offline. Bundled open-source icons, symbols, and fonts run locally and do not contact their creators. We, the developers, receive nothing about you, your child, or your use of the app.

What stays on the device

Words, categories, photos, voice recordings, settings, and a local log of which word buttons were tapped (used only to show caregivers a simple progress view inside the app) are stored locally on the device. This information never leaves the device unless a caregiver deliberately uses the Back up button, which creates a file the caregiver controls and chooses where to save or send. Removing the native app normally removes its local app data; browser-stored data can be removed through the browser or device's website-data settings.

Microphone and camera

The microphone is used only when a caregiver chooses to record their voice for a word or a name. The camera and photo library are used only when a caregiver chooses to add a photo to a word or a person. Recordings and photos are stored on the device and Nuran never uploads them. A caregiver-controlled backup includes them if the caregiver chooses to export and share it. Both permissions are optional; the app works without them.

Children's privacy

Nuran is made for children who use augmentative and alternative communication (AAC). Because the app collects and transmits no user data, no personal information about any child is ever gathered by us or shared with anyone. There are no ads, no in-app purchases, no links to social media, and no external content.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be published at this address and in the project repository before a release that changes data practices.

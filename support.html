Quick answers

No sound when tapping words? Check the device volume and the mute switch, then open the caregiver area (press and hold the "Grown-ups" button) → Settings → Sound → On. Tap one word yourself — devices sometimes need one tap after reopening before speech starts.

A word or group disappeared? Caregiver area → Recover deleted → Bring back. Nothing is ever permanently deleted by normal use.

Microphone will not record? Allow microphone access for Nuran in the device Settings. Recording is optional — without it the app speaks words by itself.

The Help alarm will not stop? Press and hold the button on the alert screen until it completes. A quick tap will not stop it — that is intentional, so a child cannot dismiss it.

Worried about losing her words? Caregiver area → Back up creates one file with everything. Save it to Files or iCloud Drive weekly. Restore rebuilds everything from that file on any device.

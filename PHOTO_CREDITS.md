# Real-world photo credits — Nuran v24

The following bounded PNG derivatives are bundled for offline AAC tiles. They are real photographs,
not generated imagery. Each derivative was resized to a maximum 640-pixel edge and stripped of
unneeded EXIF metadata. Caregiver photos remain local to the family device and are not listed here.
